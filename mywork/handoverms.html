Handover Management System

Manage shift handovers

Team members of a 24x7 project have the concept of sharing handovers with each other when the shift of a team member ends. This way the business need of 24 hours, 7 days a week is met

Handover MS is a web application that allows team members to create, read, update and mark as complete handovers shared to them. They can also add comments, documents to the handovers.

Front end build with HTML, CSS, Jinja template engine. The back end created with Django python framework. And the database was in PostgreSQL.
